Caesar Cipher

The Caesar Cipher is a very simple way to encrypt your messages. And in this entry, I'll explain how. First, imagine you want to encrypt the following message:

the quick brown fox jumped over the lazy dog

In this unencrypted (plaintext) message each of its letters map to the same letter.

A->A
B->B
C->C
D->D
E->E
...
